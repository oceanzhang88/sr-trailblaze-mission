The Fall at Dawn's Rise - Stars, Cleanse the Troubled Thoughts

Story Recap

▶ ▶ Following the advice of the Chrysos Heirs , I embarked on the trial of Time , only to learn that I had been "dead" since the moment I stepped onto Amphoreus . To reclaim the future that had been stolen from me, we pushed onward in pursuit of the Death Titan , Thanatos .

▶ ▶ Meanwhile, Anaxa , one of the Seven Sages of the Grove of Epiphany , broke free from the surveillance of the golden threads and actively sought refuge from the Council of Elders . After winning the Council's trust, he ascended Dawncloud with Cerces the Reason Titan who resided within him, and sought an audience with Kephale the Worldbearing Titan.

▶ ▶ To obtain information about the entrance to the nether realm, Castorice had no choice but to support the Council of Elders in proposing a pause in the Flame-Chase Journey. With the aid of the demigod Cipher , she set out for the ancient city of the dead dragon — Styxia .

▶ ▶ Castorice successfully recreated the dead dragon Pollux via alchemy and used its power to return to the nether realm, where she faced Thanatos the Death Titan. She returned both the Titan's Coreflame and the stolen future, restoring the cycle of life and death in Amphoreus.

▶ ▶ Anaxa's soul met Castorice in the nether realm, where he proved his hypothesis regarding creation. At the citizens' assembly, he cast a pivotal vote and used the charge of blasphemy to thwart the Council of Elders's schemes for power, allowing the Flame-Chase Journey to continue.

▶ ▶ After returning Cerces 's Coreflame, Anaxa's physical body completely disappeared. With this, the Flame-Chase Journey neared its conclusion, and all eyes turned skyward...
